Play alone or in Co-Op in this puzzle game.
Try to escape that weird place without getting blown up.
Get too close or too far from your friend and it's over.

Proximity is a game made at first during the Global Game Jam 2015. We then went to finish it and polish it further.
The game lets you play as one (or two if you play solo) of those two poor souls who are stuck in a weird place with a bomb attached on their torso.
Guide them through 15 levels but be careful, if you are either too close or too far from each other you might end up with your head blown up.
BE CAREFUL : Proximity is hard, and extreme, it's how we love games.
