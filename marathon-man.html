Bob Poztakish is a hippie living in the 70's and he want to do the first New York marathon.
For that he will use all the drugs that he could find on the way.
But beware the overdose.

This endless runner let the player discover the life of Bob Poztakish, Hippie and drug addict, as he tries to run the first New York Marathon.
While running the player will find two types of drugs, one who make him go faster and the other who slow him down.
Every time he takes drugs, a overdose bar will fill. The more drugs he takes, weirder will become the screen. If the player get out of the screen, he looses.
