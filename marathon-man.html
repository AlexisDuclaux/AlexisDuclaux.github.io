Bob Poztakich is a hippie living in the 70s and he wants to participate in the first New York Marathon.
To win, he will use all the drugs that he finds along the way.
Beware overdosing.

This endless runner lets the player discover the life of Bob Poztakich, hippie and drug addict, as he tries to run the first New York Marathon.
While running, the player will find two types of drugs, ones that make him go faster, and others that slow him down.
Every time he takes drugs, a overdose bar will fill. The more drugs he takes, the weirder the screen becomes. If he goes out of bounds, he loses.

    For SHMMNYE, I programmed the UI in C#. I also implemented every asset and animation within the engine.
