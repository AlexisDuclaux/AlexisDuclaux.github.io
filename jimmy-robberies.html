Mobile game who see Jimmy, a 80's gambler, in love with the most beautiful woman in the world.
Except she is currently dating the richiest man in the world.
Jimmy gather a team to rob all the banks of his nemesis and take his place.

Jimmy robberies was a game made at GAMAGORA school in Lyon, France, and was scheduled for April 2014 to be shown at the GAMAGORA GAME SHOW.

This 2D reflexion game made for Android put the player in Jimmy's mind, who's decided to rob all the banks of his nemesis to have his fortune and seduce his girlfriend.
Jimmy Robberies is a game who takes place in a cartoon, non-violent universe and let the player use multiples characters with multiples complementary capacities who let the player choose his own way to rob banks.
In Short, Jimmy Robberies it's : Girls, Money and sauerkraut.

In Jimmy Robberies, the player has to send at least one character of his team to the safe of the bank he's trying to rob. For that he just have to select a player with his finger and tell him what to do by giving him orders.
The player has tools, limitated or not, who gives him abilities (Like lockpicking doors, desactivate security cameras or laser that can detect him).
As the game goes on, the player will find more and more characters and objects to use for beating levels that will be more difficult.
