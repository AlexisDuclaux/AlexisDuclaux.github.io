A game about the struggles of an honest pipe repairman forced to deal with his day to day kleptomania.

You are playing as a pipe repairman hired by a supermarket to fix their pipes, but you are also a kleptomaniac man who will steal everything that is nearby.
Walk too close from an object and your character will steal it, making you heavier.
Be careful of the other customers and guards.

Instructions:

WASD to move
E to interact
Space to hold your kleptomaniac tendencies inside

    For How much control do we have over what we wish for? - An honest Game we were a team of 4, with only one programmer, one artist and one sound designer. As such I had to deal with project management, Game Design and Level Design in this quite ambitious game for a 48h game jam.
    This is one of my proudest achievements, even if ultimately we felt a bit short on the polish side.
