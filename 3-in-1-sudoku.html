3in1 SUDOKU DELUXE provides an opportunity to the player to play three variations of the sudoku game, a normal sudoku, a sudoku which replaces the numbers with diamonds and a sudoku where the numbers are placed on wheels and the player must rotate them so that the grid is aligned.

In 3 in1 Sudoku Deluxe, the player can play to three types of games:
 - A classic sudoku where the player must fill a grid with numbers.
 - A sudoku named "diamonds" where the numbers have been replaced by diamonds.
 - A sudoku named "sudo-wheels" where sudoku grids are replaced by wheels where the numbers are already marked.
 The player must then turn the wheels to align correctly in order to find the proper alignment of the grid.

    3in1 SUDOKU DELUXE and 3in1 BUBBLES are two games that I realized as a Game Designer for my 6-month internship at In-Fusio, Cenon, France.

    These 2D puzzle games on mobile phones were practically realized at the same time and were available on google play.

    For 3in1 Sudoku, I was a junior Game and Level Designer, working with another junior designer.
    For this game I had to come up with new ideas on how to improve this kind of game as well as making sure that my ideas were well defined and comunicated to each body of work.
    I also had to maintain the Game Design Document and various dictionaries.
    As a Level Designer, I had to come up with rules with which levels would then be generated.
