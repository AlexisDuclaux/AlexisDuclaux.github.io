Passionate about video games at a young age, I started to create mods for TES : Morrowind when I was 12 years old. Ever since, I've wanted to work in the video game industry.

I studied Game Design for three years in Lyon, France, after my Baccalaureat. Then, after a 6-month internship in Game Design in Cenon, I started a Level Design degree which ended in April 2014.

Since then, I've worked alongside the game industry while managing projects that integrated outside data into game engines.

To hone my skills, I often participate in Game Jams. Some of the games I worked on even won awards, for example, the game I'm most proud of, Proximity.

Download Proximity

I find joy in many different genres of video games. By not constraining myself, I find it easier to be creative when I make my own games.

I write during my free time - I am currently writing a novel and several short stories.

Do not hesitate to look at my resume and contact me if you need more information...or if you feel like it!
