Passionate about video games since my younger years, I started to create mods for TES : Morrowind when I was 12 years old.

Since that time I try to improve myself in most fields in order to work in the video game industry and understand how all the cogs in the machine works.

That's why I had studied Game Design for three years in Lyon, France just after my Baccalaureat, and then after 6 Months where I had an Internship in Game-Design in Bordeaux,

I started Level Design studies for a year (which ending in April 2014 ) still in Lyon.

Since then, I've got the opportunity to work alongside the game industry while managing projects that were about integrating outside data into game engines.

I like to design and program games and prove that often by making Game Jams, some game even won awards such as the game I'm most proud of, Proximity.

True enthusiast about RPG, space games, superheroes games and cultivating my pop-culture (Movies, Music, Comics, Games, ... ). All these things mean that I am ready to work on entirely different games with widely contrasting universes.

With the hope that one day I would be working on a fantastic RPG that would put superheroes in space, of course.

Also writer during my free time, I am currently writing a novel and several short stories.

Do not Hesitate to look at my resume and contact me if you need more information about me or just if you feel like it.
