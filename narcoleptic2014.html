Be a Narcoleptic truck driver.
Try to drive 349 Miles to go into Tennessee before you crash.
Crash into a tree, a bridge or something else and it's over.

Narcoleptic Truck Driver 2014 : Arkansas to Tennessee edition is a game made by 2 peoples for FuckThisJam, a Jam where the participants have to make a game in a genre they hate.

This contemplative game lets the player be a truck driver who have to deliver goods to Tennesse before he falls asleep.
During his trip, the driver can fall asleep and the player has to do whatever it takes to wake the driver and not crash into a tree or something else.
The player can really find the Tennessee panel if he goes all the way and drive for 349 miles.

    For Narcoleptic Truck Driver 2014 : Arkansas to Tennessee edition, we were two to deal with the game and its rules.
    Regarding the game itself, I fully realized programming in C # and all animations via the Unity game animator.
    Some graphical assets (Logo, ...) were also made ​​by me.
